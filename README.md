Grocery shopping online should be straightforward. For people with food allergies, it often isn't. Ingredient lists are long, inconsistently formatted, and packed with scientific names that most people won't recognise and no major platform, including Amazon, does anything to help. FoodFlag fixes that.

FoodFlag (this repository) is a Chrome browser extension that scans product pages in real time and automatically highlights ingredients that match your personal allergen profile. It checks for synonyms and cross-reactive counterparts that are also harmful or is known to be risky (ep. those allergic to wheat may also react to rice and corn). 

(This code is made to be unpacked within the browser extension page)
